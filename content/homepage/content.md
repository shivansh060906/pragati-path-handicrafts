Pragati Path Foundation is a non-profit organization established in 2011; it is based on feminist human right organization. Pragati Path Foundation work draws upon the inherent values of a rights-based approach to sexuality and gender equality. After noting the troubling lack of prominent feminist voices in India, PPF is established to address the leadership capacities of young women in addressing issues of sexuality and reproductive health and rights, violence against women, and social justice.


It is led by women, who identify the issues, prioritize them and propose solutions to address those issues before the appropriate authorities. The focus of our work is creating an enabling built environment and community participation in urban and rural.
